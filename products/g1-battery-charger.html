About G1 Battery Charger

12-Volt LithiumTech Charger with USB Port for Charging Devices

The 12V charger is used for charging the TheraGun's 12V battery. It has a quick charge time and allows the battery to reach a 75% charge in only 15 minutes, and a full charge in 30 minutes. A built-in USB port can charge small electronic devices, as well. The lighted on-board status indicators let you know where your battery is in the charge cycle and will turn green when the battery is fully charged. For a longer life, the battery should be removed when charging is complete.
